This is a personal blog about artificial intelligence.

In an era where AI is evolving faster than anyone can keep up with, I want to write down what I actually observe and think — not repurpose tutorials, but offer honest, first-person reflections.

What comes next? I'm not entirely sure. Maybe a deep dive into a particular AI capability, a contrarian take on where the industry is heading, or some counterintuitive finding from hands-on experience.

One thing is certain: every piece here will be something I've genuinely thought through.
